## Cookie Policy
This Cookie Policy explains how Idea Analyzer uses cookies and similar technologies.

## What are cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device to remember preferences, keep you signed in, and understand how you use the site.

## Types of cookies we use
- **Essential:** required for login, security, and core features.
- **Preferences:** remember settings like language and saved filters.
- **Analytics:** help us understand feature usage and improve performance.
- **Marketing (when applicable):** measure the effectiveness of campaigns.

## Managing cookies
You can manage or withdraw consent for non-essential cookies through your browser settings and (where provided) our cookie banner controls. Blocking some cookies may affect functionality.

## Do we use other technologies?
We may use similar technologies (e.g., local storage, pixels) for the purposes above.

## Updates
We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes to our practices. Material changes will be communicated.
